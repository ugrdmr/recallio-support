 # Privacy Policy for Recallio

  **Last updated:** January 1, 2026

  ## Overview

  Recallio is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

  ## Data Collection

  **We do not collect any data.** Recallio operates 100% locally on your Mac.

  - No analytics or tracking
  - No cloud sync or external servers
  - No account required
  - No personal information collected

  ## Data Storage

  All data (clipboard history, snippets, and settings) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your Mac.

  ## Third-Party Services

  Recallio does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

  ## Purchases

  In-app purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
